# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

## Overview

Eye Break Timer does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Stored Locally

The app stores its settings on the user's own Windows device. These settings may include:

- Work duration
- Rest duration
- Force break setting
- Windows auto-start setting
- Floating timer window position
- Local unlock password

These settings are stored locally under the user's Windows profile and are not sent to the developer or to any third party.

## Network Access

The app does not require an internet connection for its timer, reminder, rest screen, or settings features.

## Third Parties

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party data collection services.
